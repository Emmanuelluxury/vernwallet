Privacy Policy

How we protect and handle your data on the Bitcoin-Starknet Bridge

Last Updated: September 10, 2025

Introduction

At Bitcoin-Starknet Bridge, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our bridge service to transfer assets between Bitcoin and Starknet networks.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using our service, you consent to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with the practices described, you should not use our services.

Note: As a decentralized bridge protocol, we are designed to minimize data collection while maintaining security and compliance with applicable regulations.

Information We Collect

Our bridge is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide and improve our services:

Wallet Addresses

We collect and store public wallet addresses involved in transactions for operational purposes and compliance.

Transaction Data

Transaction amounts, timestamps, and status are recorded on the blockchain and in our systems for auditing and support purposes.

Network Information

We collect IP addresses and browser information for security monitoring and to prevent fraudulent activities.

We do not collect: Personal identification information, private keys, or any sensitive data beyond what is necessary for the operation of the bridge.

How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Service Operation: To process your bridge transactions between Bitcoin and Starknet networks
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraudulent transactions, abuse, and security incidents
  • Compliance: To meet regulatory requirements and prevent illegal activities
  • Improvements: To analyze usage patterns and improve our service (using aggregated, anonymized data)
  • Support: To provide customer support and troubleshoot issues

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data with partners for research or analytical purposes.

Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • All sensitive data is encrypted at rest and in transit
  • Access to user data is strictly limited to authorized personnel
  • We employ blockchain-native security practices to protect transactions
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing are conducted

While we implement robust security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but commit to following best practices in the industry.

Important: As a user of blockchain technology, you are responsible for securing your own private keys and wallet information. We never have access to your private keys.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to operate our bridge:

  • Node Providers: We use infrastructure providers for Bitcoin and Starknet node operations
  • Analytics: We use privacy-focused analytics to understand usage patterns
  • Support: We may use customer support platforms to assist users

All third-party services are vetted for privacy and security compliance. We only share the minimum information necessary for these services to function.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can request information about what data we have about you
  • Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations
  • Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: You can object to certain processing activities

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

Continued use of our services after any modifications to this Privacy Policy will constitute your acknowledgment of the modifications and your consent to abide and be bound by the modified Privacy Policy.
